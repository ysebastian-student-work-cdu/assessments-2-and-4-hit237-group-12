Tips for Reducing Food Waste

Plan your meals and make a shopping list before going to the grocery store.

Buy only what you need and avoid bulk purchases unless you know you will use all of the food before it spoils.

Store food properly to extend its shelf life.

Use your freezer to preserve food that you cannot eat right away.

Use up leftovers by incorporating them into new dishes or repurposing them.

Understand expiration dates and food labeling to avoid throwing away perfectly good food.

Donate excess food to local food banks or charities.

Use smaller plates and serve smaller portions to avoid food waste.

Be creative with your cooking and use up ingredients that are about to go bad.
