Our goal is to educate the public about the problems that arise from food waste and to provide effective mitigation strategies. We believe that reducing food waste is crucial to creating a more sustainable and equitable food system.

Did you know?

That approximately one-third of all food produced globally is wasted? This amounts to a staggering 1.3 billion tons of food that is lost or thrown away each year. This not only represents a significant economic cost, but it also has a major impact on the environment and contributes to food insecurity.

About us

Our website provides information on the various causes of food waste and offers practical solutions that individuals, businesses, and governments can implement to reduce food waste. We believe that everyone has a role to play in addressing this issue, and we hope to inspire action and change through our website.

Explore

Explore our site to learn more about the impact of food waste, discover strategies for reducing waste in your own life, and find resources for getting involved in the movement to combat food waste. Together, we can create a more sustainable and just food system for all.
